About Us

HKBK College of Engineering is a prestigious institution located in Bangalore, India. It was established around 26 years ago, and since then it has been offering quality education to students in various engineering disciplines. The college is affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), one of the most reputed universities in India for engineering education.

HKBK College of Engineering is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure and modern facilities, including well-equipped labs, libraries, and sports facilities. The college has a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are committed to providing quality education to the students.

The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Information Science and Engineering. In addition, the college also offers research programs leading to a Ph.D. degree in various areas of engineering.

Over the years, HKBK College of Engineering has established itself as one of the top engineering colleges in Bangalore, with a strong track record of producing skilled and competent engineering professionals. The college has tie-ups with leading companies in the industry, which provides students with ample opportunities for internships and placements. Overall, HKBK College of Engineering is an excellent choice for students who wish to pursue a career in engineering

Research

HKBK College of Engineering has a strong focus on research and innovation. The college has a dedicated research and development cell that aims to promote and support research activities among students and faculty members. The research activities at the college are focused on addressing the challenges faced by various industries and society.

The college has a well-equipped research lab with modern equipment and tools that enable students and faculty members to undertake cutting-edge research in various areas of engineering. The research lab is also equipped with high-performance computing facilities that enable researchers to simulate and analyze complex engineering problems.

The faculty members at HKBK College of Engineering are actively engaged in research activities and have published numerous research papers in reputed international and national journals. The college also encourages students to participate in research activities, and several students have undertaken research projects under the guidance of faculty members.

The research activities at HKBK College of Engineering are focused on several areas of engineering, including advanced materials, renewable energy, robotics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. The college also collaborates with leading research institutions and industries to undertake collaborative research projects.

In addition, the college regularly organizes seminars, workshops, and conferences to promote research and innovation among students and faculty members. The college also encourages students to participate in research competitions and has a track record of winning several national and international competitions.

Overall, HKBK College of Engineering is committed to promoting research and innovation in engineering and has a strong track record of producing competent and skilled engineering professionals who can address the challenges faced by various industries and society.
